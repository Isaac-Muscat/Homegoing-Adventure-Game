Marjorie

Yaw and Esther have a daughter and they name her Marjorie who develops a very strong bond with her grandmother, Akua. Her grandmother tells her about her roots from the cape coast castle and she exposes her to the ocean near the cape coast when she is very young. Once Marjorie returns to Alabama to go to high school, she finds herself immersed in books while suffering from racism and alienation from the other students. She is torn apart when her grandmother dies until she unites with an unexpected family member...
