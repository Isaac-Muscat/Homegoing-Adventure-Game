Yaw: Visitation

You decide to visit Edward, however, you are a little hesistant since you have not seen him in a while. When you show up at his home with Esther, you have a pleasant conversation over dinner. After you leave Edward's house, you are still tormented by the scar given to you by your mother. Throughout the following years of torment, you develop and publish a very succesful book and you live a content life with Esther.

You've reached the end of the story, but it's not your true outcome! You can do better! Back up a bit and try something different.
